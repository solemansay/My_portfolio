About Me

Hey there! My name is Soleman Sayeed, I am currently a student at UNC Charlotte. I was born in Canada and raised all over the world, I have lived in 8 countries and counting! I was raised in a big family of 7 constantly moving from city to city, school to school. I believe this is the reason that I have such good communication skills and have no problem going into a large group of people and sparking conversation. The reason that I wanted to learn how to code stems from my teen years of playing videogames and trying to figure out how they work. My first experience with coding was running mods on Minecraft and trying them with my friends. Since I started learning more I feel a larger hunger got coding knowledge than ever before!

I have many hobbies that I do outside of coding and video games, many of them including outdoor activities such as hiking, camping skiing, snowboarding, water skiing/wakeboarding, and biking. I love to travel more than anything else experiencing new and exciting cultures, eating new foods and seeing the most beautiful parts of our world. This passion of mine is a big reason why coding from home on my laptop is the perfect career for me. I also love to play my guitar and read to relax and unwind.
